# Campaign Budget Breakdown -- $500 Total

| Category                  | Amount | % of Budget | Notes                                      |
|---------------------------|--------|-------------|--------------------------------------------|
| Social Media Ads (Meta)   | $200   | 40%         | Boosted posts on Facebook and Instagram    |
| Social Media Ads (X/Twitter) | $75 | 15%         | Promoted tweet for launch announcement     |
| Book Giveaway / Contest   | $50    | 10%         | 2-3 free copies as engagement incentive    |
| Graphic Design (Canva Pro or freelancer) | $50 | 10% | Ad creatives and post visuals         |
| Email Marketing Tool      | $25    | 5%          | One month of Mailchimp or similar          |
| Amazon/KDP Ads            | $75    | 15%         | Sponsored product listing on Amazon        |
| Contingency / Misc        | $25    | 5%          | Unexpected costs or A/B test variants      |
| **Total**                 | **$500** | **100%**  |                                            |

## Rationale

- **Heaviest spend on paid social (55%)** -- highest reach-per-dollar for book launches.
- **Amazon Ads (15%)** -- captures high-intent buyers already browsing books.
- **Giveaway (10%)** -- drives organic shares and early reviews.
- **Low overhead on tools** -- leverages free tiers where possible.
